---
title: "V5Brain"
description: "Programs, files, system state, and screen operations."
---

Access this view through `device.brain`.

## State views

- `isRunningProgram` and `activeProgram`
- `battery`, `button`, and `settings`
- `cpu0Version`, `cpu1Version`, and `systemVersion`
- `isAvailable` and `uniqueId`

## Operations

- `getValue()` and `setValue()`
- `listFiles()`, `readFile()`, `writeFile()`, `removeFile()`, and `removeAllFiles()`
- `listProgram()` and `uploadProgram()`
- `captureScreen()`
- `uploadFirmware()`

<Warning>
  `uploadFirmware()` downloads and writes VEXos components. Loss of power,
  incompatible firmware, or interrupted communication can leave the device
  unusable. Keep firmware updates out of normal application flows.
</Warning>
